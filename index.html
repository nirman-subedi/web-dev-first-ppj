About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student at the Model Institute of Technology (MIT Nepal) and a frontend developer at Cagtu Nepal. I build production web applications with React and Next.js, ranging from travel discovery platforms and music streaming apps to e-commerce storefronts and operations dashboards. Recent work includes Darsan Nepal, a Nepal tourism discovery platform;,

Homaale, a task and product marketplace; Mugeet, a music streaming app; and the KoseliFoods, KoseliMart, and MithooSweet e-commerce and mart-management platforms. I enjoy turning messy data and API responses into clean, usable interfaces, and I have a background in networking, databases, and ethical hacking from my coursework.
